Pillars of "Bangladesh 2.0"

Innovation

Bangladesh 2.0 is driven by a culture of innovation, fostering a dynamic ecosystem where technology, entrepreneurship, and creativity thrive. From AI-driven industries to smart cities, we are paving the way for a future where innovation fuels economic growth and global competitiveness.

Sustainability

Sustainability is at the heart of our transformation. We are committed to building a greener future through renewable energy, eco-friendly infrastructure, and responsible resource management. Our goal is to create a nation where economic progress goes hand in hand with environmental stewardship.

Infrastructure

Modern infrastructure is the backbone of Bangladesh 2.0. With smart urban planning, advanced transportation networks, and world-class digital connectivity, we are creating a foundation for sustainable growth, enhanced mobility, and seamless global integration.

Empowerment

Empowering people is key to our progress. Through education, digital inclusion, and equal opportunities, we are ensuring that every citizen has the tools to succeed. From grassroots initiatives to high-tech industries, Bangladesh 2.0 is a nation where everyone has a voice and the ability to shape the future.

The Vision of Future

Bangladesh 2.0 aims to redefine the nations trajectory by embracing cutting-edge technology, promoting inclusive growth, and ensuring sustainability. With initiatives in renewable energy, digital transformation, and robust infrastructure, the country is set to become a global model of development.

Digital Transformation

Creating a nationwide digital ecosystem to enhance connectivity and access to services for all citizens.

Green Energy

Investments in solar and wind energy to ensure a sustainable and eco-friendly future.

Modern Infrastructure

Building state-of-the-art infrastructure to support economic growth and global competitiveness.

That collective resolve should define Bangladesh of the future and place our nation as a responsive and responsible state in the comity of nations. We must ensure that we do not miss this chance to build a new Bangladesh due to our differences. If we lose this opportunity now, we will be defeated as a nation. To the martyrs, the injured and the living students, we pledge that we will not let this achievement go to waste."

Dr. Muhammad Yunus

Chief Adviser of the People's Republic of Bangladesh
